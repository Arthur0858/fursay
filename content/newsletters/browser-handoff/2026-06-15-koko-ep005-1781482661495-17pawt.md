This Week in Koko's Forest: Boingy's Rainy Song

In this sweet forest story, Boingy loves to sing in the rain, but he feels lonely when no one joins him at first. Koko listens, brings a leaf umbrella, and turns a quiet rainy moment into a joyful game with friends. It is a lovely story for talking about sharing happiness, inviting others in, and finding fun on a rainy day.

Learning words
rain - rayn
water that falls from the clouds
Boingy sings a happy song in the rain.

share - shair
to let someone enjoy something with you
Koko helps Boingy share the fun with friends.

sing - sing
to make music with your voice
The forest animals sing together in the rain.

Parent activity
After watching, pretend it is a rainy day at home. Ask your child to open an imaginary leaf umbrella, sing one short line together, and name one way to share joy with a friend or sibling. Then say the three words together: rain, share, sing. Parent support: 看完後可以陪孩子玩一個下雨天想像遊戲，假裝撐起葉子雨傘，一起唱一句簡單旋律，再請孩子說說可以怎麼把快樂分享給朋友或手足。這樣能自然練習單字，也能延伸到同理心與分享。
