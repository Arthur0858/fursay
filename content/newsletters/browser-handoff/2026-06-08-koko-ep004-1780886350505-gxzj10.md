Koko's Forest - EP004

This Week in Koko's Forest: The Starry Night

In this gentle bedtime story, Koko feels lost in the dark forest. Gugu the wise owl helps her slow down, look up at the stars, and listen to the forest around her. It is a calm story for talking about confusion, fear, and finding the next small step.

Learning words
star - stahr
a bright light we can see in the night sky
Koko looks up and sees a star in the sky.

wise - wyz
knowing how to make a kind and thoughtful choice
Gugu is a wise owl who helps Koko feel calm.

listen - LIS-uhn
to hear carefully and pay attention
Koko learns to listen to the forest sounds.

Parent activity
After watching, turn off bright lights for one quiet minute. Ask your child to look up, point to an imaginary star, and name one sound they can hear. Then say the three words together: star, wise, listen. Parent support: 看完後可以關掉強光一分鐘，請孩子假裝看到星星，再說出一個聽到的聲音。用這個小活動陪孩子理解：害怕或混亂時，可以先停下來、抬頭看、仔細聽。
