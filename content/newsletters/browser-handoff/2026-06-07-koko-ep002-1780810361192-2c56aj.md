Koko's Forest - EP002

This Week in Koko's Forest: Helping Lili Find Her Way

In this gentle bedtime story, Koko meets Lili, a little ladybug who feels lost. The episode gives families a calm way to talk about friendship, small acts of kindness, and being brave enough to help someone.

Learning words
lost - lawst
not knowing where you are or how to get home
Lili feels lost in the forest.

friend - frend
someone who cares about you and stays by your side
Koko becomes a kind friend to Lili.

brave - brayv
trying to help even when something feels a little scary
Koko is brave when they walk along the dark path.

Parent activity
After watching, invite your child to retell the story with two small toys: one toy can be Koko and one can be Lili. Ask, "Who was lost? Who was brave?" Then let your child show how Koko helped a friend get home. Parent support: 看完後可以用兩個小玩具陪孩子重說故事，一個當叩叩、一個當亮亮。可以問孩子：「誰迷路了？誰很勇敢？」幫孩子把 lost、friend、brave 連到迷路、陪伴與幫助別人的情境。
