This Week in Koko's Forest: Meeting a New Friend

In this short forest story, Koko meets Liangliang, a little ladybug. Use the episode as a calm moment to help your child notice friendship, kindness, and brave first hellos.

Learning words
friend - frend
someone you like and care about
Koko meets a new friend in the forest.

kind - kynd
gentle and helpful to others
Koko is kind to Liangliang.

share - shair
to let someone enjoy something with you
Friends can share a happy moment.

Parent activity
After watching, ask your child: "Who was kind in the story?" Then invite them to draw Koko and Liangliang together. Parent support: 看完後可以問孩子：「故事裡誰很溫柔？誰是好朋友？」再請孩子畫出叩叩和亮亮，幫助孩子把 friend, kind, share 連結到友情、同理心與分享。
