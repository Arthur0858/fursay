🌱 Simple Reading Tips

Use one story in three easy steps.

🎧
Learn one phrase

Pick one phrase. Say it together once or twice.

💬
Ask one question

Ask: What happened? How did Koko feel? What would you do?

✏️
Do one small activity

Draw one thing, act one scene, or say the phrase before bed.
