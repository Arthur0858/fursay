Fursay Privacy

We collect the minimum information needed to run the site and story packs.

Anonymous interaction signals

We record page path, language, product ID, source, placement, interest stage, and event time to understand whether content is useful. Analytics events do not include names, phone numbers, addresses, payment data, or email addresses.

Email subscriptions

Only when you submit the free story-pack form do we send your email and chosen pack to our newsletter provider. Every email includes an unsubscribe option.

We do not sell personal data

Fursay does not sell personal data or use advertising pixels to build a cross-site personal profile.

Contact

To ask about, access, or delete subscription data, email contact@fursay.com.
