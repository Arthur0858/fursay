FAQ

Will I pay today?

No. The waitlist only records interest and opens the free story-pack signup.

You can join the weekly story pack list and choose Koko, Noor, or both inside the signup form.

When will the paid packs open?

Only after enough families click, subscribe, and ask for printable practice. The product may stay waitlist-only if interest is too low.

Yes. Email updates include an unsubscribe option, and any future paid product will show support and refund details before payment.
